Ticket Marketplace

How it works

Welcome to the ticket marketplace! Here you can purchase tickets from other attendees who can no longer attend the event.

  1. Click "Buy" to reserve the ticket and proceed to checkout.
  2. Complete your payment immediately to finalize the purchase.
     
    3. Important: Very short payment window!

    You must complete payment within 30 minutes after completing the order. Payment is only possible by credit card.

  1. You'll receive your ticket confirmation by email.
  2. We will deactivate the marketplace if we observe irresponsible scraping and botting. Please don’t ruin a good thing.

You have a ticket and want to sell it? Click the link to your order details in your order confirmation email and follow the process there.
